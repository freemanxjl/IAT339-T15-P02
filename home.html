New American Dining Experience

Taking inspiration from Korean and Japanese barbeque restaurants, COOKIN gives its dine in customers the opportunity to cook food, provided by our chefs, using our tables with an embedded barbeque grill. Our restaurant simulates backyard barbeques inside the comfort of the restaurant and with fresh, marinated and prepped foods.

We Have the Meats

COOKIN has a wide selection of meats such as burgers, hotdogs, steaks, and marinated chicken wings. We also provide fresh vegetables such as onions and peppers to grill. Though our main appeal is American food, we also have food from other cultures such as kebabs, and Korean bulgogi. Can't dine in? We sell raw, prepped meat for you to still enjoy our food in the comfort of your own home. Please see the menu page for more info.
