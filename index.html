Company Branding Idea

What the business does

A restaurant which seats people on specially made tables featuring a barbeque grill. Customers buy prepped meats and vegetables to cook using the grills on their table. Customers can make reservations for tables. People also have the option to buy the prepped food to take home. It's exactly like kbbq but with American food such as burgers, wings, etc instead

How the business is presented

The brand's persona will be somewhat informal but still professional so it is suitable for most audiences. No overly casual or country jargon/speak. Stay proffesional but accessible to retain as many users as possible.

Why people would be interested

This restaurant would bring people in for a unique eating experience, good food and a great time to spend with people. Also gives people the barbeque experience for people who cannot have a barbeque in their own home.
